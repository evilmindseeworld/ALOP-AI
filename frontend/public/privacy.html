ALOP-AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ALOP-AI, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, name, and avatar from Clerk authentication.
  • Chat data: messages, prompts, generated images, and screenshots you share with the overlay assistant.
  • Usage data: number of messages, images generated, and plan status.
  • Payment status: subscription status, Stripe customer ID, and Stripe subscription ID. We do not store full payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI chat, image generation, and screen analysis services.
  • Manage your account, subscription, and usage limits.
  • Improve service performance and fix errors.

3. Payment Information

We use Stripe to process payments for Pro subscriptions. We do not store full credit card numbers or bank details. We only receive your subscription status, Stripe customer ID, and Stripe subscription ID.

4. Third-Party Services

We share limited data with services required to operate ALOP-AI:

  • Clerk — authentication
  • Supabase — database and storage
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — frontend hosting
  • Render — backend hosting
  • Google Gemini — AI vision and text processing
  • Brave Search — real-time web search (Pro users)

5. Screenshots and Overlay Mode

When you use the desktop overlay assistant, screenshots are sent to Google Gemini for analysis. We do not store screenshots on our servers unless required for debugging.

6. Data Retention

Chat messages are stored until you delete them or your account. Usage data is retained for plan enforcement. Payment records are managed by Stripe.

7. Your Rights

You may delete your account and chat history at any time by contacting us or using the in-app delete options.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: alopai@example.com
